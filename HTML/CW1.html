Welcome to India, a diverse and culturally rich country.

Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of lights , is one of India's most celebrated festivals.

Holi

Holi, the Festival of Colors , is known for its vibrant celebrations.

Eid

Eid is a significant Muslim festival marked by prayers and fasting.

Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Jaipur

Jaipur , often referred to as the Pink City, is known for its historic sites. including:

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerela

Kerela is famous for its serene backwaters and lush green landscapes.

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is creamy and flavorful Indian Dish

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic rice dish with various regional variations.Notable ingrediant include:

  1. Basmati Rice
  2. Chicken or Lamb (or vegitable for vegitarian)
  3. Spices
  4. Yogurt

Dosa

Dosa is a South Indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
