Overview

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the best batsmen of the modern era. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli has achieved remarkable success in international cricket

Kohli has won several prestigious awards, including the ICC Cricketer of the Year (Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy) multiple times, and has been named among the top batsmen in the ICC rankings
